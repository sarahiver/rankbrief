What do these numbers mean?
Clicks – How often someone clicked on your result in Google.
Impressions – How often your site appeared in search results.
CTR – Of 100 people who saw your result, X% clicked on it.
Avg. Position – Your average rank in Google (1 = top).
Engagement Rate – Share of visits with real interaction (scroll, click, ≥10 sec).
Missed Clicks – Extra clicks you would get at 5% CTR (industry average).
