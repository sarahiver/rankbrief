What do these numbers mean?
Clicks – How often someone clicked your result in Google.
Impressions – How often your website appeared in search results.
CTR – Out of 100 people who saw your result, X% clicked.
Avg. Position – Your average ranking in Google (1 = top position).
Besuche (Sessions) – Total number of website visits. One person can have multiple visits.
Unique Visitors – How many unique people visited your website.
Engagement Rate – Share of visits where someone actively engaged (scrolled, clicked, or stayed 10+ sec).
